Facts About Registered Massage Therapists in Ontario

  • Your Massage Therapist in Ontario is a regulated Health Care professional who has received two years of comprehensive training in anatomy, physiology, and health sciences.
  • Massage Therapy in Ontario is regulated under the RHPA (Regulated Health Professionals Act), the same governing body that oversees nurses and physicians. This requires that your Massage Therapist conducts his/her practice in a way that offers his/her clients a safe and professional experience.
  • Most extended Health Care companies cover Massage Therapy in part or in whole.
  • If you plan to claim your Massage Therapy treatment, please contact your insurance company and find out whether you need a referral from your doctor in order to be reimbursed.
